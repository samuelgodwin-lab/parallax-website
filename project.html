Project Overview

A digital experience
worthy of the badge.

BMW's existing digital product suite had outpaced its design language. Dealer tools, configurators, and customer-facing portals were each built independently — no shared system, no coherent experience. The brief was to unify them.

We built a complete design system from the ground up: token-based, component-driven, documented at every level. Then engineered the flagship configurator using it — fully responsive, accessible to WCAG 2.1 AA, and performant at sub-100ms interaction latency.

The Challenge

Seven products. Zero shared foundations.
Everything built in isolation.

Each product team had made independent decisions — different component libraries, different token systems, different interaction patterns. A user moving between the configurator and the dealer portal felt the seams. BMW wanted those seams invisible. That required more than visual consistency; it required a shared engineering foundation that every team could build on.
